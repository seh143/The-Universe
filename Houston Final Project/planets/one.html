55 Cancri e

Discovered August 30th,2004, 55 Cancri e is nicknamed “Hell on Earth” for its dystopian conditions. It is an exoplanet orbiting the Sun-like host star 55 Cancri A. Its mass is about 8.63 Earth masses and its diameter is twice that of earth. It was the first “Super Earth” type exoplanet to be discovered orbiting a main sequence star. It orbits its star once every eighteen hours. The star facing side of 55 Cancri e can reach more than 2,000 Kelvin, which is hot enough to melt iron. The backside is estimated to be a temperature of 1,644K on average. It is unknown whether the planet’s composition is mostly carbon-rich or oxygen-rich materials. Its atmosphere could contain elements similar to Earth’s atmosphere with elements like oxygen and nitrogen.
